Open License

For a long time, copyright laws have been improved to further restrict the right of the public to distribute, copy or share contents of different kinds on the internet. These contents may include, articles, art, poetry, graphic designs, musical lyrics and compositions, softwares.

What are Open Licenses?

Without a special license, these use and distribution of intellectual properties are normally prohibited by copyright, patent or commercial license. This is where open license comes in. Open Licenses are legal and binding contracts between the user of an intellectual property and the author. This legal binding declares that the property can be used in commercially under specified conditions. The license is what turns the property into an open component. In the case of sotwares,Without an open source license, the software component is unusable by others, even if it has been publicly posted on GitHub.

Why user an Open license?

Open licenses enable creators to allow more freedom in what others can do with their works. Benefits of this freedom include:

  • allowing others to circulate the work freely - potentially giving it a greater circulation than if a single group or individual retained an exclusive right to distribute;
  • encouraging others to continuously improve and add value to a work
  • encouraging others to create new works based on or derived from the original work - e.g. translations, adaptations, or works with a different scope or focus



Whether you wish to keep your work completely proprietary or you wish to file for patent protection. Take time to understand the Terms of Use agreements and choose the open source license that is the best fit for you
